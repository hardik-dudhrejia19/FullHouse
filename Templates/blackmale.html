Synopsis

We present to you the official trailer from the upcoming Bollywood movie "BlackMail". Gulshan Kumar & Ramesh Deo present, T-Series & RDP Motion Pictures Film, Produced by Bhushan Kumar, Krishan Kumar, Abhinay Deo and Apurba Sengupta, BlackMail is directed by Abhinay Deol. The film has Irfan Khan and Kirti Kulhari in lead roles along with Divya Dutta, Arunoday Singh & Omi Vaidya. The movie is releasing on 6th April 2018.
